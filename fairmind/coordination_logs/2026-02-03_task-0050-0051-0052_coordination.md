## Key Technical Decisions

1. **Debounce Timing**: 1 second for auto-save (balances UX and performance)
2. **localStorage Keys**:
   - 'anonymous-form-draft' for form data
   - 'anonymous-form-steps' for step states
3. **Draft Expiry**: 7 days (matches existing persistence utilities)
4. **Step States**: 5 states (completed, active, visited, available, locked)
5. **Progress Calculation**: Dual metrics (completion % and position %)
6. **Navigation Permissions**: Can go back freely, can go forward to next or visited sections only
7. **Validation**: onBlur mode for React Hook Form (less intrusive)
8. **Section Completion**: Any field with data marks section as "has progress"
